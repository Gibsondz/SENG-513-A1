Welcome!

League of Legends has 5 different types of roles that are part of the game. This website was designed to provide brief descriptions of each role so you can make the informed choice about which role you should start playing when you play the game. Below is a quick description of each to get you started. From there you can use the tabs for more detailed descriptions.

Descriptions
Role Description
Top Tanky bruisers and fighters, initiate fights for your team and play the front line.
Jungle Fight against mosters in the jungle instead of other players, impact multiple parts of the map and gank enemies when they least expect it.
Mid Wield magic to destroy your enemies with powerful spells or be an assasin and kill your enemies in the blink of an eye.
Marksman Weak earlygame with strong late games. Attack fast, play well, and be the carry your team needs.
Support Do as the name suggests, support. You don't deal much damage but you make up for that in being able to crowd control enemies and heal/shield teammates from damage.
